## Understanding Liquid Ketamine
Liquid ketamine, often in the form of ketamine hydrochloride solution, is a medication primarily used in medical settings for anesthesia and pain management. It's a dissociative anesthetic with potential therapeutic applications, such as treating depression in controlled clinical environments. In 2026, its use continues to evolve under strict medical supervision due to its Schedule III status in the US and similar classifications globally.

Ketamine's liquid form is typically administered via injection or infusion by licensed professionals. For non-medical purposes, acquisition is heavily restricted to prevent misuse.

- **Veterinary Applications**: Commonly used in animal care.
